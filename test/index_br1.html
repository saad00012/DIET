About Us

Shaping Minds,
Building Futures

Dnyanshree Institute of Engineering & Technology (DIET), Satara — a well-known engineering institute founded in 2012 by the great visionary Hon. Chairman Mr. Dnyaneshwar B. Wangde (Bhai) under Raosaheb Wangde Master Charitable Trust (RWMCT).

  • AICTE approved, DTE Mumbai approved, DBATU Lonere & MSBTE affiliated institution with consistent academic excellence.
  • Modern, well-facilitated campus with state-of-the-art labs, AI/Data Science centre, and Mitsubishi Automation Skill Development Centre.
  • University toppers consistently produced since inception — dedicated to holistic student development.
  • Industry partnerships with Red Hat, AWS Academy, Mitsubishi Electric, and 12+ companies through formal MOUs.
